Character Creation

In Age of Claritas, players assume the role of "Adventurer." This is an unofficial title granted to those with heroic potential. Both skill and judgment are tested as characters face various trials in unique situations. This role-playing system presents challenges that are built to support and reward players' individual strengths as well as their ability to work as a team.

How to Use the Character Creation Guide

For the best experience, utilize this guide on a tablet or computer.

  1. Make a copy of the Online Character Sheet Template or Download a Word Document version.
  2. Keep this page open, work alongside it, and fill in each field unless prompted not to.
  3. Use the popup links on the page for quick access to relevant information for each field.
    • If you would rather not view this information in a popup link, you can navigate through the "Rulebook" menu and select the desired page.
  4. Complete all fields.
  5. Once finished, submit it via the email link and print a copy to bring with you to the event.
  6. Congratulations! You just made a brand new character in the world of Claritas. We are excited to meet them!

This will be decided by your Kin. Choose one you can start with.
Techniques:
Techniques are earned in game. Most classes start with none, but Warriors start with a Title.
Skills: Abilities:
Choose 1 free Weapon Dexterity. Select Magic Spells if you are a Wizard.
With 15 XP, select up to 3 R1 Skills and Abilities. Wizards get 1 R1 spell in 1 School of Magic.
