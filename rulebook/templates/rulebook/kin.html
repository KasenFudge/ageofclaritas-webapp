Kin

A Kin is the ancestry of a character. A character's Kin will decide their appearance and will influence how the character will act.

Attributes

Every Kin has unique Attributes (listed within each Kin's Page). Players may select one to start with and may learn two more by completing Kin Quests during the course of an event. (Players cannot start with attributes marked by an *asterisk).
